SDU Life

Everyone is involved in the development of student life.

Clubs offer good time and development in their field, and students, in turn, develop the club itself and present them on stage in front of other clubs.

By spending time in clubs, students find friends, love, and possibly themselves. Who did they imagine themselves to be all this time.

Therefore, clubs play a very important part in the life of students.

SDU Debate Club - Panem

Public speaking in the Debate mode develops the ability to think and speak at the same time, develops creativity and wit of speech. In Debates, it is necessary not only to clearly formulate your theses and arguments, but also to refute objections and convincingly answer the questions of opponents.

SDU IQ Club - Sana

This club helps students develop their logical thinking, which in general will help in solving many difficulties in the future. The meeting discusses various tasks and problems.

SDU Club - Puzzle

Puzzle club offers you: intellectual rest from study, logic development for the brain, fitness for the Mind, childhood nostalgia with different types of games, large-scale events, including a lot of students!
